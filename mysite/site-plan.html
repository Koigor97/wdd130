Overview

Purpose

Every computer programmer/developer, be it front-end or back-end have a personal website as an online presence, apart from the usual social media platforms like Twitter, Instagram, Facebook and so on. And on that note, I also want to establish an online presence in the form of a Personal Portfolio website. The purpose of this website is to state what I do, my educational background pertaining to the field, my experiences, the technologies I know, projects that I've worked on personally and with a team, and awards I would win as my career progresses. This make it easier for recruiters/empolyers and individuals to access me, and see what I am all about and what I can bring to table with my skillsets.

Audience

My targeted audience are recruiters and future employers. However, it also for anyone who wants an online presence. It can be a blog website, a landing page with information of the client, start-up e-commerce store, or a web application.
