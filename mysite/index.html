About Me.

Hi again, my name is Samuel Fogbawa Turay but I go by my native name Koigor. The name Koigor means "Figth the War" in my native language "Mende". It was given to me my grandparent because as a baby I survived the 10 years Blood Diamond Civil War of my country. I'm a Programmer from beautiful small country called the Republic of Sierra Leone. Population of 8.3 million. It is the home country of British actor Idris Elba and German football star Antonio Rudiger. I presently live in South Africa. I love technology, it's fascinating what it can do. I love knowing how things work, and love building things. I am a hardworking man, humble, friendly, and competitive team player. I love photography and videography, love documenting life. 80s music are my jam, love animation movies and good love story, like long drive and a good adventure. If you have an idea on a website or web application and want to bring to life, I am your guy.
