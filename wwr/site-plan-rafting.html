Overview

Purpose

When people want to get away to relax and have fun, they go on the internet and search for places within there city/state, or even outside there city/state where they could get that freindly, fun packed, competitive, adrenaline pumping range of activities. Having an online presence like a website, makes it possible for a lot more people to know about the club, and what activities we offer. By doing this, we channel a lot customers, build relationships, increase revenue, and become an household name.

Audience

Our target audience are Families, Couples, Teenagers, Young adults, and Companies. Some of them would be working, and some would have their own businesses (be it start-ups or, already established) ranging from different industries. Now these people want to do something fun, thrilling, that will keep them on their toes, something new, they want to be out in nature and have the time of their lives, create memories with their families, spouses, and friends. And these are the amenities we provide. Since nowadays everyone have smartphone, tablet or laptop, they can easily access our website.
